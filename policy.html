Privacy Policy - Briteema Inventory Management System

Our privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our inventory management system.

Information we collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Information about your inventory, such as product names and quantities
  • Demographic information such as your location
  • Other information relevant to our inventory management services

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our inventory management services to you
  • Communicate with you about your inventory and related matters
  • Customize our inventory management system to better meet your needs
  • Comply with any legal or regulatory obligations

Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We have put in place suitable physical, electronic, and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information we collect online.

Disclosure of information to third parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. However, we may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in providing our inventory management services to you.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
